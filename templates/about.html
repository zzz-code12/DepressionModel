About This Project

Student Depression
Prediction System

An ML-powered tool designed to help identify signs of depression in students.

What is this?

This dashboard is a Student Depression Prediction System built with Machine Learning. It analyzes key lifestyle, academic, and personal factors to assess whether a student may be exhibiting signs of depression — offering a fast, accessible first step toward awareness and help.

The Model

The prediction model was trained on a dataset of student mental health records using Logistic Regression. Here's a summary of the model's performance:

Input Features

Input features include academic pressure, CGPA, sleep habits, dietary patterns, suicidal ideation history, financial stress, work/study hours, and family history of mental illness.

How to use it

Navigate to the Prediction page and fill in the form with honest responses about your current lifestyle and academic situation. The model will analyze your inputs and return a prediction on whether you are showing signs of depression.

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer: This tool is not a substitute for professional mental health diagnosis or treatment. The results are for informational and awareness purposes only. If you or someone you know is experiencing mental health difficulties, please consult a qualified healthcare professional or counselor.
